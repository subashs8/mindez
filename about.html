The Youth of Today is The Future of Tomorrow

Due to the stress from school and various other factors, there has been a rise in mental health issues among students. At such a delicate phase it is important that students receive the emotional support and guidance they require in order to succeed.

Why Does It Happen?

As a society, it is important to take charge of addressing the emotional and mental health issues of those of the younger generation. The reasons students face these issues are not difficult to find. Broadly they are:
Stress is caused by:
  • Worry over school grades and performance
  • Managing changes in ones body
  • Financial difficulties in the family
  • Aggressive schoolmates or teachers
  • Frequent change of houses or schools
  • Racist taunts and bullying
  • Pornography use
  • Substance abuse
Anxiety that shows in:
  • Excessive worry
  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Stomach upset
  • Trembling
  • Shortness of breath
Depression manifesting as:
  • No interest in normal activities
  • Eating trouble
  • Low energy
  • Low self-esteem
Family problems such as:
  • Parents divorce
  • Abusive parents
  • Lack of understanding of a students needs
These stresses manifest in many ways in the student, like
  • Reduced appetite
  • Lack of sleep
  • Physical illnesses
  • Aggressive behavior
  • Being socially withdrawn
  • Poor academic performance
  • Not participating in group activities
  • Always complaining or blaming
  • General unhappiness
While parents and teachers can notice some of these and help in minor ways like providing a sense of security to the student, lending an understanding ear or encouraging the student to take part in social activities like sports and arts, there is only so much they can do. When stress symptoms persist, it is a sign to have a counselor intervene.

Get the Support You Need Right Now

Unlike many other illnesses of the body, there is an unwanted stigma associated with mental illness. Even our culture was for long insensitive to the mentally ill and tended to make light of their challenge. Words like crazy are loosely used to shame those who are essentially just sick people needing attention.

Mental illnesses range from mild anxiety and depression to more serious issues like bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, obsessive compulsive disorder, dementia, and psychosis. But in order for a minor ailment to not flare up into something serious, it is important to address it early. This is because some illnesses can be degenerative, some require medication, some in-patient institutional care, etc. Counseling is certainly advised if you begin to have doubts about consistent worries plaguing your students mind. You could be nipping a potentially chronic problem in the bud.

A Tailored Approach to Success

We know that every student is unique which is why students are assigned to a counselor depending on their needs. For more convenience, students can access these services through video calling, audio calling, or chatting.

The five steps to success

1. Find your right counselor

Chat with the counselor and precisely identify your mental health needs. There are no one-size-fits-all solutions in the world. You as a young individual, are unique. You deserve sole special attention and from the right expert too.

2. Decide on a payment plan to suit your budget.

3. Begin message communication with your counselor at your convenient time and location.

The counselor assigned to you will be available to you whenever you need counseling. You can either chat, do an audio call, or even do a video call whenever needed. The counselor will be available to respond to your messages 1 or 2 times a day. You can carry on the dialogue in this private channel.

4. Undergo the healing process

You can seek to redress your emotional and other mental health issues in the course of the counseling sessions. You may not be aware of your issues but your counselor can unearth them for you in a clear, non-judgmental way. Once the problem is pinpointed, a focused approach to addressing it becomes easier.

5. Transform into a new individual

Our purpose is not just to give you quick-fix solutions, but to deliver long-term health benefits. At the end of the day, you must feel the transformation in you. When that happens, Mindez would have achieved its objective.

So many ways to receive the support you need

Interact online

Initially, and for most of the interaction, you will be using the online chat facility for getting in touch with your counselor.

You can initiate a live chat session with your counselor by scheduling a time. At the scheduled time, log in to your account to start typing and chatting with your counselor.

Talk on the phone
You can initiate a phone call with the counselor and expedite the communication process. After you schedule a session with the counselor, the system will request your phone number. The system will call you at that number and connect you with your counselor to start the phone session.
Meet and chat
Video chat sessions help put a face to the name and save time through ruling out chances of miscommunication. Once you schedule a video chat with the counselor, you may wait for the counselor to prompt you to start the session.
Email your thoughts
Regardless of the immediate availability of your counselor, you can email him using the facility in Mindez. Rest assured you will get a prompt and understanding response.
The communication channel
Mindez provides a private and secure communication channel for the student and the counselor. Whatever you write, chat or convey by any means will stay on this channel. You can ask questions and seek guidance in real-time or otherwise. When the counselor is offline and you have emailed him, you may wait for the notification indicating his response.
