Why should schools take an interest in the students mental health?

Students spent most of their waking hours in the school and therefore the institution has a major responsibility in ensuring their holistic health. It is imperative that the schools provide programs to promote social-emotional development, prevent the occurrence of mental health and psychosocial problems.
