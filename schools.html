Schools

Why should schools take an interest in the students mental health?

Mental health and stress problems are often the major factors that affect a students school performance. Students spent most of their waking hours in the school and therefore the institution has a major responsibility in ensuring their holistic health. It is imperative that the schools provide programs to promote social-emotional development, prevent the occurrence of mental health and psychosocial problems. They need to put in place programs that intervene as early as possible to reduce casualties. It is not that schools are totally indifferent to the cause now. They have been taking a number of measures. However, the programs are fragmented and isolated with hardly any coordination between them. But by leveraging a powerful app like Mindez, schools can get a handle on their students progress through the method of engaging them with the right professional counselor.

How do we ensure privacy?

Mindez app is all about ensuring the safety and privacy of the user information. Here are the ways in which it is brought about:
  • You do not need to identify yourself.
  • All that you convey to your counselor is protected by strict federal and state laws. We do not share your data with any agencies.
  • The chat communication between you and your counselor is securely encrypted by banking-grade 256-bit encryption.
  • Our databases are encrypted and scrambled to insure against theft or abuse.
